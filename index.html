LT4CPR @ AACL 2026

Language Technologies for Crisis Preparedness and Response (LT4CPR) focuses on speech and language technologies that support crisis preparedness, disaster risk reduction, and crisis response—especially in settings with linguistic diversity, low-resource languages, and informal communication channels. Our goal is to bridge NLP, local languages, and real-world humanitarian impact.

The workshop features invited talks and a panel discussion on strategic priorities and shared infrastructure for the community, and includes a shared task on automatic situation report (“sitrep”) generation from crisis-related social media content.
