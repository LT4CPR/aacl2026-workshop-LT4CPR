LT4CPR
Language Technologies for Crisis Preparedness and Response
November 9, 2026  ·  Hengqin, Zhuhai, China  ·  Hybrid
Where language AI meets crisis operations

A one-day workshop co-located with AACL-IJCNLP 2026, bringing together NLP researchers and crisis management practitioners to advance language technologies for disaster preparedness and response. Featuring invited talks, a panel discussion, and a shared task.

Topics of Interest
  • Social media monitoring and narrative analysis
  • Volunteer and community coordination tools
  • Crisis detection and early warning systems
  • Machine translation in humanitarian contexts
  • Automatic sitrep generation
  • Translating NLP research into operational tools
  • Information extraction and situational awareness
  • Ethics and societal implications of AI in crisis
Shared Task
Automatic Sitrep Generation

Generate structured situation reports from crisis-related social media. Dataset and baselines coming soon.
